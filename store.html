Welcome to the CREATE Store

We sell affordable electronic components and robotic parts to all students and staff of UNSW. You may visit our pop up stall or shop online here.

We also operate a vending machine outside the MCIC Makerspace which can be used at any time.
We cannot guarantee that stock is available.

Stall Opening Hours

Every Monday from 12pm to 4pm during UNSW academic teaching periods only (not including summer term).
