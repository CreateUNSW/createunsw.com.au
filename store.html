Welcome to the CREATE Store

We sell affordable electronic components and robotic parts to all students and staff of UNSW. You may visit our pop up stall or shop online below.

We also operate a vending machine outside the MCIC Makerspace which can be used at any time. We cannot guarantee that stock is available.

Email sales@createunsw.com.au for all enquires.

FAQ

I need an urgent pickup, how fast can I pickup the order?

Send us an email to organise a time to collect.

Can I pickup my order outside UNSW or via delivery service?

This service is primarily for UNSW students and staff only. However we may fulfil bulk orders from outside UNSW

Stall Opening Hours

Every Monday from 12pm to 4pm during university academic terms only (not including summer term).

Location

Globe Lawn

In rainy weather the stall will be moved to Michael Crouch Innovation Center

Keep up to date on our stall locations on our Facebook Page.
