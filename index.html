What We Do

Workshops & Socials

CREATE runs various workshops & social events throughout the regular university terms. No formal arrangements are necessary to attend the workshops, just show up and join in. Learn More

Electronic Component Sales

CREATE sells electronic parts including micro-controllers, breadboards, Arduino kits and quadcopters throughout the regular university terms. Learn More

Projects

CREATE actively participates in various public projects including Vivid Sydney Light Festivals and Droid Racing Challenge (DRC). Learn More

Funding for Student Projects

If you have an idea for a project that involves putting together and making any project, we want to provide you with everything needed to get your project off the ground. Learn More
