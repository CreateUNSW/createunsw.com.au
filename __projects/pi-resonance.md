Resonance is an interactive light installation that invites visitors to engage with the living memory of place.
The work is inspired by the name Kogarah, a Dharug word meaning "place of reeds”, celebrating the nearby native flora and wetlands.
Touch the grasses and a ripple of light travels through the entire field. At its centre, an infinity well offers a moment of quiet reflection.
